Farmers Around the World

Smallholder Farmers

Approximately 500 million smallholder farms worldwide produce a significant share of the world's food, often using family labor and traditional knowledge.

Sustainable Practices

Many farmers are adopting methods like crop rotation, agroforestry, and integrated pest management to protect soil and biodiversity.

Challenges Faced

Farmers face climate risks, market volatility, and access to credit and inputs. Supporting local markets and fair trade helps resilience.

Technology & Innovation

Digital tools, mobile advisories, and improved seeds are helping farmers increase yields and reduce waste, especially when training and access are available.
